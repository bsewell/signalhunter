First Product

DigestEQ

Nine years of undiagnosed GI issues. A spreadsheet from a dietitian that was too hard to use consistently. That gap became the app.

The Problem

Bathroom behavior isn't something you talk about. Symptoms come on slowly. It feels like a nuisance, not a medical problem. So it stays hidden. For years. ER trips. Ruling out what it isn't. Finally a GI dietitian hands you a spreadsheet. You can't use it at 3am. You can't use it at a restaurant. You stop logging. The mystery continues.

The Insight

Every other system was built for the brain. Give the person a chart. A score. A spreadsheet. DigestEQ is built for the moment. The 3am moment. The restaurant moment. Capability before comprehension. The person needs to feel like they can log — before they can understand what they know.

Voice-First Logging

The keyboard is the last resort, not the primary input. One tap, speak, done. The fastest possible path from "something happened" to "it's recorded." Designed around BJ Fogg's principle that every extra step is a dropout risk.

GPT-4 Vision Meal Analysis

Take a photo of your meal. GPT-4 Vision identifies ingredients, estimates FODMAP load, flags trigger foods (lactose, gluten, fructose, allium, cruciferous), and predicts expected symptom delay in minutes. This is running in production now, not a demo.

Weekly InsightEngine

Every Sunday at 8pm, the InsightEngine analyzes 14 days of data. Meal patterns, bathroom timing, meal-to-symptom lag, medicine effectiveness. The output is one sentence: "Last three Tuesdays were rough — you had onions each time." Not a chart. A finding.

Studio Console Design

The amber-on-black aesthetic isn't for coolness. It's armor. When you're logging bathroom behavior for the first time at age 30+, you need a frame that's clinical, not clinical. The console metaphor turns "I have an embarrassing medical problem" into "I'm debugging my body's signal."

The Operator

The app's voice. 337 lines of rules. Never fires snark after a first occurrence. At pain level 9, the persona suspends entirely — just warmth and support. Garlic-infused oil gets: "The fructans stayed in the pan. Solid call." It's persona-based content routing, gated by user engagement level.

Architecture for Independence

All writes go through an EventRepository protocol. The backend can swap from Google Sheets to Supabase to CloudKit in a single line of code. Offline-first with ULID-based event IDs. This isn't theoretical — the DualWriteEventRepository already exists for the migration.

Current Phase: 1.5

Status

All five log types (bathroom, meal, symptom, medicine, insight) submitting on device. GPT-4 Vision enrichment live. InsightEngine running weekly. Next gate: TestFlight to five internal testers, then App Store submission.
